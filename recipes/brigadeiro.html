Brigadeiro

Description

A traditional brazilian, chocolately treat served at birthdays and holidays. Incredibly simple to make with even simpler ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons of unsweatened coco
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 (14 ounce) can of sweetened condensed milk

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan over medium heat, combine cocoa, butter and condensed milk.
  2. Cook, stirring, until thickened, about 10 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat and let rest until cool enough to handle.
  4. Form into small balls and eat at once or chill until serving.
