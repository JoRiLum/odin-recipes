Tacos

Description

A delicious, Americanized version of one of Mexico's most well-known meals. In this recipe we have chosen to use hard shells for their satisfying crunch.

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 can of beans: baked, black, or pinto.
  • 1 yellow onion
  • store bought taco shells, any brand.
  • 1 pack of shredded cheese
  • 1 bag or head of romaine lettuce
  • Taco seasoning of choice

Steps

  1. First, get a medium sauce pan and empty the beans of choice into it. Fill the can with water and pour into the same pan. Head saucepan at medium.
  2. Take the shells and arrange them evenly on a baking tray and place them in oven. Set oven to 400 dgeree fahrenheit.
  3. Place beef into skill pan and turn heat to medium. Mix with a spatula until meat is thoroughly brown. Depending on the fat percentage of the beef, you may have to strain it before proceeding.
  4. Add seasoning of your choice into beef along with 3/4 cup of water. Turn heat to low and mix beef until water is almost completely boiled away.
  5. By this time your beans should be cooked, shells should be crunchy and all ingredients should be ready to mix into delicious tacos.
  6. Mix beans and meat together and spoon them into your shells. Add whichever topping you like. Don't forget the cheese!
